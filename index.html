About e-Karamchari

e-Karamchari simplifies internal employee services for the Municipal Corporation of Delhi by providing a unified digital platform. It significantly reduces paperwork, streamlines administrative processes, and empowers employees with self-service capabilities for leave management, salary information, and grievance redressal—making government operations more efficient and transparent.

Key Features

Online Leave Management

Apply for leave, track status, and manage your leave balance with ease.

Salary Slip & Service Record Access

Download salary slips and access complete service records anytime.

Attendance Tracking

View your attendance records and monitor your work hours efficiently.

Grievance Redressal System

Submit and track grievances with a transparent resolution process.

Role-Based Access Control

Secure access with role-specific permissions and data protection.
